VESSELS ARENA || THE PLACE OF INTIMACY

Intimate Vessels Church

The divine visitation of Miss Kathryn Kuhlman caused quite a stir in me till I stumbled into Hebrew 12:23 “…the general assembly and church of the firstborn who are registered in heaven, to God the judge of all , to the spirit of just men made perfect.” It dawned on me that God employed this means- the spirit of just men made perfect, to communicate to me what he had in stock for my calling; abive all, to chart a spiritual course for similitude in ministry which is hinged on the holy spirit. The December 4, 2001 vision built on this land and gave me a clearer direction as the signpost written and raised by the Angel read “Intimate Vessels’ Church”. God wanted me to raise in nations vessels that will walk in fellowship, communion and intimacy with the holy spirit (2nd Cor.13:14), thus useful for the master and the prepared for every good work (2nd Tim. 2:21).

But in a great house there are not only vessels of gold and silver, but also of wood and clay, some for honor and some for dishonor.

2 Tim 2:20
