Cause for which I live is built on my relationship with jesus. I first gave my life to Christ in 1988, in the Old scripture Union I got baptized in 1992, in Living Word Ministries. Characteristically, in 1994 the inward fire kindled by genuine salvation kept me on the move as I crisscrossed my environment hunting for souls. This became a lifestyle for me till 1996, June 19 to be precised, when I had an opened daylight vision, which formed the nucleus of what stands today as Intimate Vessels’ Church. A sort of high voltage passed through my body. My eyes were fixed on the doors as someone walked in. initially I didn’t see the person clearly the closer the person came, the clearer.

I recognized the person by miss Kathryn Kuhlman, arrayed in a long white gown possessing tiny gold droplets and a long white hair. Her soft misty eyes were fixed on me as she smiled towards me. Miss Kuhlman was a healing evangelist of the 40s down to the early 60s. Her international Ministry, in the world of Roberts Liardion laid a foundation for the workings of the holy spirit’ in her dispensation and beyond. Standing in front me, she didn’t utter a word, neither did I reached out to touch her, but I couldn’t. To the best of my ‘knowledge’, she stood in front of me for “ages”. At a point she turned and left with a broad smile. Indeed, December 4, 2001 brought me into another place of epoch. At this point the finger binding me “come” took a firm outlook. I shared this encounter with my then fiancé who is now my wife – Pastor Angela, and confessed to her that I wasn’t ready to do Church. The components of the demand didn’t elude me much because I’ve served in this capacity for some time, but I just wasn’t ready to pioneer any work. At such, I waited for another 4years to get clearer directions and connect the dots of his leading. By December 2005, the church was inaugurated, and officially kicked off in January 2006.
